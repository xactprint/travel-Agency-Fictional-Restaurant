Our Story

A Labour of
Love & Fire

La Casbah was born from a simple conviction: that food, when prepared with intention, becomes a form of communication. Founded in 2009 by Chef Karima Abidi, our kitchen has always been a place of quiet discipline and joyful creativity.

Nestled in the heart of Algiers's (Alger Centre), we source exclusively from small producers and family farms within 150 km of our door. The seasons dictate our menu — never the other way around.

Whether you join us for a weekday lunch or a celebratory dinner, our promise remains unchanged: every detail, every flavour, every moment — executed with care.
